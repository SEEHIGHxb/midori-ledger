🌿 Quick User Guide

Welcome to Midori Ledger, your minimalist workspace for elegant financial tracking. Learn how to master its leafy accounting features:

🕰️ Virtual Time-Travel

Click the top green calendar badge or use the step buttons to change dates. Future scheduled items generate automatically, and returning to the past will dynamically filter them out.

💳 Multi-Currency Sync

Wallet cards show balances converted to the active Base Currency, plus their original local values. Changing the base currency at the header instantly updates all metrics.

📈 Flexible Budgets

Taggings can be selected to be included in the Budget Panel via checkboxes. Specific monthly and yearly limit thresholds can be configured directly from the Budgets tab.

📅 Recurrence Rules

Setup automated salaries, recurring bills, or subscription deposits under the Schedules tab. You can optionally enforce custom expiration bounds via End Occurrence dates.

ZenSync Cloud Synchronization 🍃

Link your PC and mobile devices securely. All data is encrypted on your device using AES-GCM-256 before upload. Nobody, including the storage broker, can read your ledger without your private Sync Key.

Cloud synchronization is currently Disabled. You can enable it to back up your ledger or pair with another device.
